Kyle Barrentine

Kyle Barrentine is studying Psychology at North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State university. He is very involved within the psychology department and coordinates events for other aspiring psychologists to explore their field and prepare for graduate school. He is a R.I.S.E scholar, who is currently researching the pathology of depression in older adults in the B.R.A.C.H (Behavioral Research on Aging, Cognition, and Health) lab under Adrienne-Aiken Morgan. His main research interest is how depression develops across the lifespan, and addressing disparities within rates of diagnoses. He aspires to be a Professor of Psychology with a focus in developmental psychology.
